HELLO, This is Communities

Communities will be a Global Social Media Platform specializing in connecting people back to their cities and everything happening around them. We believe our platform will exponentially increase the vibrance and the dynamics in any city.

Communities will make sure that people witin a City will have the ability to share ideas and connect on a more personal level. This will drive local growth and inspiration, unleashing the human spirit and new possibilities around the globe
